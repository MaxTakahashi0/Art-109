The game of DOOOOM!!!!

A lil' bit of rock never hurt anyone, eh?

This project was my first attempt at using Substance Painter, and I decided to challenge myself by trying to create a character that could feasibly be realistic, while still leaning into a clay-like style. (Also it was definitely a good excuse to flesh out this oc that has been rattling in my brain for a bit). Throughout the process, I learned a fair bit of knowledge about rendering, post effects and lighting setups. This was also my first time trying out high poly sculpting, normal baking, and retopo.

Lots of firsts for this one! Besides those, I feel like this project helped me get a full hold on managing curve-based hair, and also helped me brush up on muscular anatomy.
